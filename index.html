Your Bike Shop

--Sales and Service--

When you walk into either the Riverside or Warren location you are stepping back in time. You will enter a small business and talk directly to the owner, salesperson, and service person of the best bicycle shop(s) in Rhode Island. We have kept our operation of sales and service of bicycles small so we may keep our overhead low (to pass the savings on to the customer) and so we may provide the best service possible to our customers.
